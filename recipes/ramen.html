Maruchan Top Ramen

Description

This is a recipe for Maruchan Top Ramen. I decided to do this instead of a "real recipe" because I don't really know how to cook and this is the best I can do. Take or leave it bud. It's still pretty good though!

Ingredients

  • 1 Ramen cake.
  • 1 Packet of seasoning.
  • A pinch of Cayenne pepper.
  • A pinch of salt.
  • 2 cups of water.

Steps

  1. Place a pot on the stove and pour in 2 cups of water. Turn stove to medium heat and bring water to a boil.
  2. Grab ramen package and crush the cake inside.
  3. Once water is at a rolling boil, open ramen package and dump the crushed ramen cake into the water.
